About Us



Hi! We are Lego Bionic Fusion (LBF) and we are 5 kids that are currently participating in the First Lego League Competition (FLL). FLL is a world wide competition where teams of kids get to compete in three categories: Robot, Project, and Core Values. For more information, visit the FLL Website. Our names are… Shannon, Pauline, Emmy James, and Shaunak. We are currently all 8th graders attending Miller Middle School. This year is James and Shaunak’s 5th year in FLL, Shannon’s 4th year, Emmy’s 3rd year, and Pauline’s 1st. Our hobbies include eating food… and that’s basically it. ENJOY OUR WEBSITE!!!
